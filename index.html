Alchemy

2015

Unity 4

C#

Personal work

You are an alchemist, and you have to manipulate and combine elements to create potions.

My goal was to have a world and a gameplay as 'physical' as I could: any object can be moved by the player, ingredients are slowly cooked, evaporated, mixed... Fires emit heat around them depending of how hot they are, how far the objects are from them, etc.
